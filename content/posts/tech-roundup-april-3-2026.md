## Microsoft's $10B Japan Bet

Microsoft will invest 1.6 trillion yen (~$10B) in Japan between 2026 and 2029, targeting AI infrastructure and cyber defense.

> "AI infrastructure is becoming a state-level strategic priority."

## SpaceX's Record IPO Plans

SpaceX is courted anchor investors for what could be the **largest IPO in history** at $75 billion. Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund is being courted for a $5B anchor position.

### Why It Matters

A SpaceX IPO of this size would reshape public markets and reset expectations for how late-stage AI companies finance enormous ambitions.

## Pentagon vs Anthropic

The Trump administration appealed a federal judge's order blocking the Pentagon from taking punitive action against Anthropic. The case centers on efforts to label Anthropic a supply-chain risk.

## Monster Take

Three stories. Three levels of reality shift. Microsoft building AI as national infrastructure. SpaceX raising sovereign-scale money. The Pentagon fighting an AI company. Welcome to 2026.
