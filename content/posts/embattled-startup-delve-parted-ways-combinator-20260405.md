## The Lead

Embattled startup Delve has ‘parted ways’ with Y Combinator. The controversy around Delve appears to have cost the compliance startup its relationship with accelerator Y Combinator. Delve is no longer listed among YC’s directory of portfolio companies, and the Delve page seems to have been removed from the YC website. In addition, the startup’s COO Selin Kocalar posted on X that “YC and Delve have parted ways.”

“I still remember the day we took our YC interview at MIT,” Kocalar said. “We’re so grateful to the community and every founder friend we’ve made.”

YC isn’t the first investor to distance themselves from Delve. Insight Partners also appears to have deleted posts about its investment in the company , although its primary blog post was later restored.
